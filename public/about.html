Rebalancing tool

Whether you are looking to invest some extra cash or retooling your portfolio, our tool will help you understand how many securities you need to buy or sell to achieve your target allocation.

How to use our rebalancing tool?

Determine the investment amount

Before making investing decisions, consider the amount you are comfortable investing.

Identify the securities to invest

Perform a thorough research of the securities to invest into before using the search bar to select the ticker or company.

Provide some historical information

To provide you a wholistic assessment of your portfolio, provide historical details of the amount and quantity of investments currently held.

Enter your target allocation

Carefully consider your risk tolerance and goals to determine a target allocation for each security.

Calculate your results

Sit back and let our tool crunch the numbers before reviewing the results of your portfolio.
