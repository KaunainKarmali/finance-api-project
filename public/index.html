Assumptions and limitations

Calculations are based on the most recent closing price, which may vary from real time prices.

Due to API limitations, our tool can calculate the prices for 5 securities each minute.

Our tool can only be used to search for securities listed on the NASDAQ or NYSE.

All amounts are expressed in USD.

Your results may be rounded to the nearest whole number.
